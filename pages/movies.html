Featured Movies

Inception (2010)

Genre: Sci-Fi, Thriller | Rating: 9.5/10

Directed by Christopher Nolan, Inception explores the world of dreams within dreams. Dom Cobb, a skilled thief, is offered a chance to have his criminal history erased if he can perform "inception"—the act of implanting an idea into someone’s subconscious.

The Shawshank Redemption (1994)

Genre: Drama | Rating: 9/10

Based on a novella by Stephen King, The Shawshank Redemption tells the story of Andy Dufresne, a banker wrongfully convicted of murder. Inside Shawshank Prison, he forms a friendship with Red, a longtime inmate, and eventually finds hope and freedom in the most unexpected ways.

The Dark Knight (2008)

Genre:Action, Crime, Drama| Rating: 9.5/10

: Another Christopher Nolan classic, The Dark Knight showcases Batman’s battle against the Joker, Gotham City’s most chaotic villain.
